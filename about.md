## What is the CTO-Agent?

The CTO-Agent is an experiment in building an AI-powered technical leadership assistant — a "Codex" that encodes operational knowledge about the tools, services, and workflows a CTO interacts with daily.

At its core sits a **Skills Registry**: modular plugins that give the agent capabilities like querying GitLab, searching Jira, pulling Salesforce data, reading 1Password secrets, and posting to Slack or Telegram.

## Who writes this?

I'm **Daniel Albertini**, CTO at [Anyline](https://anyline.com). This blog documents the journey of building the CTO-Agent — the technical decisions, the dead ends, and the surprisingly productive moments of human + AI pair programming.

## Why a blog?

Because building with AI is still new territory. The patterns aren't established yet. Writing forces clarity, and sharing might help others navigating similar paths.

Every post mixes technical depth with honest narrative — what worked, what didn't, and what surprised me along the way.
