## Becoming a Cyborg

The CTO-Agent is an experiment in building an AI-powered technical leadership assistant using Claude Code. It encodes operational knowledge about the tools, services, and workflows a CTO interacts with daily, all accessible from a single terminal. It knows everything about Anyline, our products, our customers, our codebase. It helps me make better decisions, focus on the right topics, know where to follow up and where to go deep.

## Who writes this?

## Why a blog?

Because building with AI is still new territory. The patterns aren't established yet. Writing forces clarity, and sharing might help others navigating similar paths.

Every post mixes technical depth with honest narrative. What worked, what didn't, and what surprised me along the way.
